Terms of Service

About Us

Omega Bot was created to assist server owners in managing their ever-growing communities. With moderation commands, utility systems, fun games and so much more, Omega Bot aims to be the all-in-one Discord bot to suit your every need. And if it doesn't? You can easily send your feedback and ideas to the Omega Team!

Omega Bot is updated monthly with brand new features, commands, and webpages. The bot is also completely free to use with an easy setup process and a 24/7 support team to help you with any questions you may have.

Usage

Through usage of Omega Bot, you accept Omega's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Additionally, you accept Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines, these of which are enforceable through Omega Bot. We will send reports to Discord about you breaking their terms if and when deemed necessary.

Rules

1) Spamming
No spamming the API or Omega Bot's commands in an attempt to crash the Service.

2) Illegal
No using Omega Bot for illegal (by UK law) and/or unethical practices.

3) Discord Terms
No using Omega Bot to break the Discord Terms of Service.

4) Locking
No locking Omega Bot's Service behind your own paywalls.

5) Bigotry
No using Omega Bot to promote racism, sexism, homophobia or other forms of bigotry.

6) Adverts
No using Omega Bot to promote your own services and/or Discord servers.

7) Rumours
No starting harmful rumours about Omega Bot or unecessary drama in our servers.

8) Exploits
No exploiting bugs in Omega Bot's code.

Contact

The easiest way to contact the Omega Team is via our Discord support channels. In our server, you can directly contact the bot developers and managers for quick and easy support.

Another way to contact us is via our email, support@omegabot.xyz. This email is for direct contact with the managers of Omega Bot and should not be used for general support as response times may be slow.
