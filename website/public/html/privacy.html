Privacy Policy

About Us

Omega Bot was created to assist server owners in managing their ever-growing communities. With moderation commands, utility systems, fun games and so much more, Omega Bot aims to be the all-in-one Discord bot to suit your every need. And if it doesn't? You can easily send your feedback and ideas to the Omega Team!

Omega is updated monthly with brand new features, commands, and webpages. The bot is also completely free to use with an easy setup process and a 24/7 support team to help you with any questions you may have.

Access to Data

Data is only permitted to Omega Bot's developers, and only within the scope required for the development, testing, and implementation of Omega Bot. Any data collected is not sold, shared, or provided to any third party, except when it is required by law or a Terms of Service agreement. To request the viewing or deletion of any data collected by Omega Bot, see the Contact section of the document.

Storage of Data

Data collected by Omega Bot is stored in a MongoDB database. This database is secured to prevent external access and there are regular security procedures in place to protect said data, however no security guarantee is provided and the Omega Team assumes no liability for the unintentional or malicious breach of data. In the event of an unauthorised data access, you will be notified via the Omega Bot Discord server (It is highly recommended that all users of Omega Bot join this server).

User Rights

You have the right, at any time, to request to view and delete the data pertaining to their respective Discord Accounts. To request the viewing or deletion of any data collected by Omega Bot, see the Contact section of the document.

Underage Users

The use of Omega Bot is not permitted for minors under the age of 13, or under the age of legal consent for their country. This is in compliance with the Discord Terms of Service. No information will be knowingly stored from an underage user. If it is discovered that a user is underage we will take all necessary action to delete the stored data.

Contact

The easiest way to contact the Omega Team is via our Discord support channels. In our server, You can directly contact the bot developers and managers for quick and easy support.

Another way to contact us is via our email, support@omegabot.xyz. This email is for direct contact with the managers of Omega Bot and should not be used for general support as response times may be slow.

Definitions

Definitions of capitalised words on this page can be found in this section of the document.

"Omega Bot" or "bot"
The Discord bot these terms are in reference to.

"Omega Team" or "we"
Referring to the people that develop and/or manage Omega Bot.

"you" or "your"
You, the user of the service.

"service" or "services"
The service that Omega Bot provides.

"data"
The information that Omega Bot collects.
